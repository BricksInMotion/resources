Windows software

Find Windows software for every stage of brickfilming!

Pre-production

  • Celtx

    A free pre-production package which aids in the screenwriting, note making and storyboarding of any film.

  • LibreOffice

    A free, open-source software suite comparable to Microsoft Office.

Production

  • Anasazi Stop Motion Animator

    Anasazi is a simple and basic animation capturing software that saves and plays back your animation on your hard drive.

  • Dragonframe

    The industry standard software for stop motion animation.

  • Helium Frog

    Professional stop motion software with many features.

  • MonkeyJam

    Although it is designed for pencil and paper, MonkeyJam can also be used for stop motion animation and has several features just for that. MonkeyJam is one of the most popular softwares among the community, and is particularly helpful when using the QuickCam Pro 9000.

  • StopMojo

    A free animation suite.

  • Boats Animator

    A stop motion animation program created using NW.js and WebRTC.

Post-production: Video

  • Axogon Composer

    Axogon Composer is a free video effects program made to create quality videos at broadcast quality. This freeware can also composite.

  • Cinefx

    Cinefx is an offline playback, editing, encoding, animation and visual effects tool based on the Jahshaka technology that allows you to create professional digital media on your desktop. Use the tools that hollywood uses to create blockbuster films and special effects.

  • CineGobs Keyer

    Easy to use chroma keyer, color difference keyer and difference keyer.

  • Debugmode Wax 2.0

    A free compositing, editing, and visual effects package. Considered somewhat buggy (make sure you back up your project regularly), but very powerful.

  • GIMP

    GIMP is the GNU Image Manipulation Program. It is a freely distributed piece of software for such tasks as photo retouching, image composition and image authoring. It works on many operating systems, in many languages.

  • Lightworks

    An exceptionally powerful and versatile professional video editing package, recently released for free to the public. (This was the software used to edit The King's Speech.)

  • LSMaker

    LSMaker is a simple free tool for making lightsabers and other basic effects.

  • Papagayo

    Papagayo is a lip-syncing program designed to help you line up phonemes (mouth shapes) with the actual recorded sound of actors speaking.

  • VirtualDub

    Free software for processing video files (specifically AVIs). In addition to the built-in functions (frame manipulation, deinterlacing, etc.) there are hundreds of plugins available: colorize, crop, rotate, blur, adjust speed, etc.

  • Windows Movie Maker 2.1

    Build your movie with a few simple drag-and-drops. Delete bad shots and include only the best scenes. Then share your movie via the Web, e-mail, or CD. Using third-party software you can even take movies you've made and turn them into DVDs. You can also save your movie back to the video tape in your camera to play back on a TV or on the camera itself.

  • Windows Movie Maker 2.6

    Newest Version of WMM for Vista or Windows 7.

Post-Production: Audio

  • Audacity

    Free audio editor and recorder.
