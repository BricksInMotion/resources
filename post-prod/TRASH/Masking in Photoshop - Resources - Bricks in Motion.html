Masking in Photoshop
Article by Matthew Bowler (Edit/Delete/Back to draft)

Here is a tutorial on how to mask in Photoshop CS5 Extended.

Hello, once you have opened Photoshop open the image with no rig and minifigure in it.
Once you have done that make a new layer called foreground.
Once you have done that open the image with your rig and minifigure in it.
Then rub out the rig, you may have to zoom in.
Now all you have to do is do this for every frame in your film that has a jump in it.
